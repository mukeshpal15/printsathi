Invitation cards

An Invitation card is a spoken or written request to someone for coming to an event or party for a meeting or a meal, it is printed paper which is printed according to the occasion like, Birthday's, marriage's, special wedding anniversaries, graduation party, etc.
There are different Tips to write an invitation message: Address of the receipt,
Relevant details about the time, venue and date of the occasion
Purpose of the event
Extent a polite invitation
Use formal and informal language according to the occasion
The formal structure of invitation card It comprises such details as who, whom, where, time, and the purpose of the invitation. Other important details like the name of the organizer and address.
